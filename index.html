Beauty and the Beast

Characters Visuals Information

Belle

Belle is a beautiful, intelligent, and genuine young lady. She loves reading and writing stories. She is also the daughter of Maurice, an inventor in a small French village.

Beast

Beast is a chimera, which is a mixture of different animals and has very beasty features because of a spell put into him. He is harsh, angry, and violent, but love made a way to open his warm side in order to break the spell.

Gaston

Gaston is whom fell inlove with Belle at first sight. He is filled with jealousy and insecurity. He also decided to marry Belle because she was the most beautiful girl in his eyes even when he did not truly love her.

Lumière

Lumière is a kindhearted, charming, and suave candelabra which was turned into one because of the spell. He sees himself as a lady's man and displays emotion.

Cogsworth

Cogsworth is a strict, proper, and good-natured enchanted pendelum clock which was turned into one because of the spell. He is the Beast's head butler and is in charge of giving orders to the other servants.

Chip and Mrs. Potts

Chip and Mrs. Potts have a mother and son relationship of table appointments. They were turned into a teacup and teapot because of the spell. They make sure of the castle's housekeeping and figure of advices.
